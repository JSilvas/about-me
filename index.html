Journal Entry 1 - So Developer, Much Learning

Feb. 6th, 2018

We're two days into the code fellows 201 course and I'm already feeling my brain stretching a bit. Day two covered a lot of material, and it's forcing me to push against my own limits for practical application. The part about all of this that really strikes me as quite different from my previous experiences is career focus. In so many other education environments (outside the military, of course), you learn concepts and theory most of the time, and rarely are you actually applying this new knowledge to the real world.

Today I found myself butted up against the wall of my own mental blocks. Thus far, planning out the structure of things that seemed so immaterial and abstract as code was a huge barrier for me. I would study and research on a topic for many hours, until I was able to converse relatively well about it for a layman. Eventually, I've often run up against the wall of practically applying the knowledge to a complex task, like building a website. This pit me against that mental block and I'm actually relatively happy with the results from just a few hours of hacking away on it.

I'm not really sure why it has been such a struggle to achieve a relatively organized grasp of so many of these concepts, but it finally feels like many of the pieces and their purposes are falling into place, at least in my mind. I suppose we'll see how well that continues to translate into tangible work to show for my efforts. In the meantime, I plan to post more actively on Twitter and perhaps try to write a few articles to post online relevant to my experience here. Onwards into the deluge!
